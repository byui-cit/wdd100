LDS Online Responsibilities Essay

Activity Instructions

Estimated Time: 30 minutes

Step 01

Read or listen to Elder Ballards “Sharing the Gospel Using the Internet” .   

The document is available here:

http://lds.org/ensign/2008/07/sharing-the-gospel-using-the-internet?lang=eng&query=Sharing+Gospel+Using+Internet

Step 02

After reading Elder Ballard's talk, write a 2 page (a title page and two content pages) essay that answers the question, "What are our responsibilities as Latter-day Saints in regards to sharing the gospel through the internet?"

The essay must be typed, double spaced, a separate title page with your title and your name/class info, and a body of content that is minimum of 2 full pages.  Make sure that you check your essay for spelling and grammar errors.  You will find a word template with a title page already attached... please use the template for your essay: 

(download and save the template to type your essay)

  LDS Essay Template

Step 03

You must include your final word count in parentheses at the end of your essay (on the last page at the bottom).

Step 04

Go to the next page to access "Wk12 LDS Online Responsibilities Essay" and submit your essay.

Grading

This essay will be graded using the following rubric (10pts):

Using the essay template, your essay must show the following:

  • must be typed, double spaced
  • have a separate title page at the start with your title and your name/class info
  • have a body of content that is minimum of 2 full pages. 
  • Make sure that you check your essay for spelling and grammar errors.

Please submit in Word or PDF format (no zip files please).
