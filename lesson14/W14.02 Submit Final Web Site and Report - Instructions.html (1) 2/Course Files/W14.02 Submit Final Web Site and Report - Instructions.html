Activity Instructions

Step 1 (75pts - 25 pts per page)

For this part of the final project, make sure to review any feedback you have received on your DryOar website and make any corrections or enhancements to make sure it looks and everything functions properly.

Make sure all images display and that all links either work (send the user to where it should. Make sure there are no spelling errors . Make sure that your header contains: logo, nav, and banner on all 3 pages.  Make sure that your footer is on all 3 pages and contains your copyright/name info and the working social media icons.

For review here are the requirements:

The following are the specific requirements of the website: The website must consist of a home page and two 2 child pages. The website must be published to the web (it already is through Handcraft).

The main page of your website must satisfy the following requirements:

  • Significant content consisting of both text and a minimum of one image (in addition to the banner image). This page must be set up as each lesson instructed and laid out according to weeks 9 and 10 instructions.
  • Working links to two child pages and social media links to the actual website
  • Be visually appealing and readable (colors, fonts, images, layout....)

Each child page of your website must satisfy the following requirements:

  • The same complete header and footer as the home page
  • Significant content consisting of both text and a minimum of at least one image (in addition to the banner image).
  • These pages may be set up the same as your home page, or you may make them any layout you choose, but you must have some layout (Not just a list of headlines, paragraphs, and images thrown on the page with no placement via css).
  • Working links to the other child page and home page and social media links to the actual website
  • Be visually appealing  and readable (colors, fonts, images, layout....)

Step 2 (25pts)

For this part of the project, you are required to type a final report using Word detailing the design and development process of your completed website.

Your report must have:

  • title page 
  • minimum of 2 full pages of content of the document 
  • must be double spaced and size 12 font

The following is a description and an outline for the final report:

Final Report (15 pts):  Final Report Template  to use for making the final report.

Title Page (2 pts)

  • Separate Title page with your Title, your name and class, and the date.

Introduction (3 pts)

  • Introduce the subject of your website
  • Explain who the audience is for of your website
  • Include the URL for your completed website

Goals/Objectives (2 pts)

  • Explain what you hope to accomplish with this website

Design Process (6 pts)

  • Describe any major design changes you made to your website as you were completing the development process (i.e. does your website look and work the same as when you first envisioned it?)

Conclusion (2 pts)

  • Describe what you learned from completing this project and contemplate how these skills may help you in the future.

Updated Site Plan (10 pts)

You have already created this in week 3.  Make sure you update your wireframe to reflect any changes you made in your text, colors, logo, images, or navigation pages.  Update your home page wireframe and remove the instructions at the top of the wireframe page.  Add your other two wireframes that you made in week 11 for your two sub-pages to the end of your Site Plan .

  • Site Plan MUST have a Title page with your title, name, and class info
  • Your updated Site Plan (created in week 3). It will be updated with the final colors, text, site map, and logo if changed
  •  You will have 3 updated and complete wireframes (home page, child page 1 and child page 2) at the end of your Site Plan.
  • Make sure that each wireframe image fits on ONE page for each wireframe
  • Make sure that you have removed the directions at the top of the wireframe page and any other instructions that you may have forgotten to remove.
