Activity Instructions

Step 01

For this part of the project, make sure to review any feedback you have received on your DryOar website and make any corrections or enhancements to make sure it looks and functions great. Make sure all images display and all links either work or do nothing. Make sure there are no spelling errors as well.

For review here are the requirements from the syllabus.

The following are the specific requirements of the Web site: The Web site must consist of a main page and at least 2 child pages. The Web site must be published to the web. The main page of your Web site must satisfy the following requirements:

  • Significant content consisting of both text and a minimum of one image. You need to make sure this page has the appropriate content for your audience so that they can understand the meaning and message of this site.
  • Links to child two pages
  • Be visually appealing
  • Each child page of your Web site must satisfy the following requirements:
    • Significant content consisting well formatted text. You need to make sure these pages have the appropriate content for your audience so that they can understand the meaning and message of each page.
    • Links to your main page and at least one of your other child pages
  • Be visually appealing

Step 02

For this part of the project, you are required to write a final report detailing the design and development of your completed Web site. The following is a description and an outline for the report

Introduction (12 pts - 4 pts for each required subtopic)

  • Introduce the subject of your web site
  • Explain who the audience of your web site is
  • Include the URL for your completed Web Site

Goals/Objectives (3 pts)

  • Explain what you hope to accomplish with this web site

Design Process (5 pts)

  • Describe any major design changes you made to your web site as you were completing the development process (i.e. does your web site look and work the same as when you first envisioned it?)

Conclusion (5 pts)

  • Describe what you learned from completing this project and contemplate how these skills may help you in the future

Step 03

Submit your final website by making sure that everything is up to date in your host. Go to the next page to access "W14.01 Submit Final Web Site and Report" and click "Upload" to upload a word document. (100 pts)
