Completing the Wireframe

Step 1

A wireframe is a series of boxes, text, and images on a page that shows how your page will look before you begin creating the page with html and css for the internet.  You will be using Moqups to work on your wireframe.  

The first thing you will need to do is to create a Free account on Moqups using your byui.edu account.

  OR    

One wonderful thing about Moqups is once you have a free account, you can email them and they will give you a free premium education account... free while you are with byui.edu.

Once you have your moqups account, you are ready to begin.  On the activity page, look at what the home page looks like before you begin.
