Activity Instructions

Estimated Time: 20 minutes

We are learning about the importance of planning when building a website. How much more important do you think planning your education is? The university has a Graduation planning tool for you to use to help with this that we are going to dive into this week.

Here are a few resources for you to use while completing this activity.

  • Grad Plan Guide Video (Video)
  • BYU-Idaho Degree Audit (Video)
  • Grad Planner Tool
  • 2015-2016 BYUI Course Catalog

Step 01

Login to your MyBYUI page and complete your graduation plan for your chosen major. Once completed take a screen shot and post the image to ILearn.

If you have not yet declared a major then you are considered an Interdisciplinary Studies/University Studies student. You can still complete the assignment.

If you have already completed your graduation plan...then just spend a moment to make sure it is still accurate, then take a screenshot, submit it, and enjoy the extra time you don't have to spend on this activity. :)
